About
Sarthakjain.live

This website is a design project developed by Sarthak Jain

A documented portfolio has become and outdated way of sharing a profile, where platforms like Dribble and Medium provides services to share artwork and designs to the community, I think developing a website takes my profile to a whole another level.

I wanted a platform where I could share all my work experiences and content at one place. So I have designed this website to share my artboards and video content at the same place.

On a personal Blog I will be share my ideas and perspective on my own platform where people can learn new things about graphic desiging and new trends of designing.

Looking for great opportunities, I will be updating the UI of this website very soon and hopefully you will get a more interactive experience.
